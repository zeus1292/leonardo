## AI PM Job Market (as of early 2026)

### Who's hiring AI PMs
- AI-native startups (seed to Series B) — want builders who can go 0-to-1
- Enterprise SaaS companies adding AI layers (Salesforce, Workday, ServiceNow)
- Big tech AI divisions (Google DeepMind, Meta AI, Microsoft Copilot)
- Vertical AI companies (fintech, legaltech, healthtech with AI)

### What they're looking for
- Hands-on experience shipping AI features (not just roadmapping)
- Understanding of LLM evaluation, RAG, agentic systems
- Ability to work directly with ML engineers
- Enterprise experience a plus for B2B roles

### Akshay's positioning edge
- Rare combo: technical depth (RAG, agents, Python) + 10 yrs PM experience + enterprise (S&P) + startup (C3 AI)
- Has shipped AI products, not just managed them
- Columbia MS + CS undergrad = credibility with technical teams
